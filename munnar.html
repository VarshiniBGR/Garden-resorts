In all the tourist spots in Munnar, you can witness the murmurations of nature in every season.

You can take a leisurely stroll along the tea gardens on balmy Munnar evenings, or for adventurous trekking, book a jeep ride through the rugged terrains of Kolukkumalai Peak and the view is a sight to behold.

Take home a memorabilia of Munnar.

  • TEA GARDENS

    Visitors to Munnar can immerse themselves in the world of tea through guided tours of the plantations. These tours provide a fascinating glimpse into the tea-making process, from the meticulous plucking of the leaves to the elaborate steps of processing and packaging. Tea tasting sessions allow you to savor the distinct flavors, from robust black tea to delicate green and aromatic white teas.

  • CHINNAR WILDLIFE SANCTUARY

    Relax in our well-appointed accommodations, surrounded by lush gardens and the sounds of nature. Explore the rich biodiversity of Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary, home to rare species like the Grizzled Giant Squirrel and Indian Star Tortoise, with guided safaris and treks. After a day of adventure, unwind with delicious local cuisine and enjoy the peaceful ambiance of our resort.

  • TOP STATION

    Top Station is famed for its stunning vistas, where you can witness the mesmerizing sight of the clouds floating below you, creating a magical atmosphere. The view stretches across rolling hills, valleys, and forests, making it a photographer's paradise. On clear days, you can even catch a glimpse of the distant town of Kodaikanal in Tamil Nadu.

Awaken to nature, unwind in comfort.
